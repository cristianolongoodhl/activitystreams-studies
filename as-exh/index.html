Some Additional Constraints for Activity Streams

as-exh is an extension of the OWL encoding of Activity Streams. As-exh provides additional constraints aiming to provide a precise encoding of the descriptions provided in natural language in the Activity Vocabulary.

Try out the consequences of these proposed constraints by running a reasoner on the following examples.

  1. Page items are items of the parent collection.
  2. Collection pages may be themselves paged.
  3. The first, current and last page of a collection are parts of the collection.
  4. Prev and next are each other inverse.
  5. Not Consistent: the first page of a collection must have no predecessor.
  6. Not Consistent: the last page of a collection must have no successor.
  7. Not consistent: Question objects can't have both anyOf and oneOf.
